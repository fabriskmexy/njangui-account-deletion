# Njangui Account and Data Deletion

Last Updated: June 8, 2026

Users may request deletion of their Njangui account and associated personal data.

To request deletion, send an email to:

fabriskmexy@gmail.com

Subject:
Account Deletion Request

Please include:
- Your registered email address.
- Your full name.
- The community associated with your account.

Upon verification, we will delete or anonymize your personal information within 30 days, except where retention is required by law or for fraud prevention.

Data that may be retained temporarily includes:
- Financial transaction records where legally required.
- Audit logs necessary for security and dispute resolution.
